About the Company

CareSure Insurance is a healthcare-focused insurance company portal designed to help patients review insurance services, understand their claims support process, and check their balance amount in a simple way.

This demo web application suits your insurance and denied-claims project idea by including a secure login, an about-company page with navigation, and a patient area to check outstanding balance information.
